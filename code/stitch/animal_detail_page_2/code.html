The Atlas Lion, also known as the Barbary Lion, is a population of the lion subspecies Panthera leo leo that was native to North Africa, including the Atlas Mountains. It is currently considered extinct in the wild, though possible descendants survive in captivity, including those here at the Morocco Virtual Zoo.

Fun Fact

lightbulb

These lions were once the largest lion subspecies, known for their exceptionally dark and long manes that extended over the shoulder and down to the belly.
